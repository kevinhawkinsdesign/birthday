The Plan

A week of everything

We'll be based at Masia Can Brull with days full of good food, open air, and whatever mood strikes.

🥾

Hiking

Montseny Natural Park is right on our doorstep — trails through oak and beech forests, waterfalls, and sweeping views of the Osona valley. All levels welcome.

🦌

Hunting

The mountains around Brull are classic hunting country. Whether you're an old hand or a first-timer, we'll have days out in the field to look forward to.

🏊

Swimming

Cool off in the pool with cold drinks, good conversation, and zero agenda. The height of an August afternoon in Catalonia.

🍷

Wine Tastings

Catalonia produces world-class wine and cava. We'll explore nearby bodegas and enjoy long lunches with bottles of something excellent from the region.

⛳

Golf

Club de Golf Montanya is minutes from the masia — a scenic 18-hole course designed by David Thomas, set in the hills with stunning mountain backdrops.

🌑

Total Solar Eclipse

On August 12, a total solar eclipse crosses Spain at sunset. Drive ~2 hrs to Tarragona for totality, or watch a stunning deep partial eclipse from the masia. One for the books.

🪵

Just Chilling

Long dinners on the terrace. Evening fires. Slow mornings with coffee and nowhere to be. The best kind of week.

Getting There

Getting to El Brull

The masia is 1 hour north of Barcelona, 2.5 hours from Madrid. Most guests will fly into Barcelona and rent a car for the drive.

From Barcelona 1 hour

Fly into Barcelona–El Prat (BCN), one of Europe's major hubs with flights from most major cities.

Rent a car at the airport. Drive north on C-16 toward Puigcerdà, then follow signs toward El Brull & Montseny.

Scenic mountain drive. Stop for coffee if you'd like. Arrive at Hotel Montanyà by late afternoon.

🚗 Book your rental car early — August fills up fast.

From Madrid 2.5 hours

Fly into Madrid–Barajas (MAD), Spain's largest airport with excellent international connections.

Rent a car. Take the A2 northeast toward Barcelona, exit for Guadalajara, then head toward Montseny on local routes.

Longer drive but doable in a day. More scenic once you hit the mountains. Grab lunch en route.

🚗 Direct flights from North America to Madrid are often cheaper.

From Elsewhere Flexible

Barcelona is the easiest hub. Fly into BCN and follow the Barcelona route.

Budget flights often connect through secondary European hubs — Paris, London, Rome. All are 1–2 hours from Barcelona.

Once in Barcelona, rent a car and drive to El Brull. It's a lovely road through the Catalan countryside.

✈️ Book early for the best flight rates and car rental availability.
